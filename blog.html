Hey guys welcome to my blog

MAY 20 2020

Hey there folks, Mike Fulham here. You all know that license plates have been a lifelong passion of mine, so I finally made the leap and started this blog, as an outlet for my passion and interest for this fascinating subject. I will be keeping all you folks posted with weekly newsletters keeping you in the loop of the wirlwind that is license plates. You can also check in for the much heralded license plate of the day, and play the highly entertaining Number Plate Connections! Have fun, and hope to be seeing you soon!

- Mike
