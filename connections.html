Rules of the Game

Number Plate Connections is the classic interactive game where you test your mental fortitude against the game's mighty license plates. There are 16 (sixteen) license plates in this grid, and they can be arranged into 4 groups, each group based on colour or car make. However, don't think it's that simple! Some cars may be able to fit into multiple categories, meaning you have to figure out what the true solution is! Have fun enjoy guys! hahahahahahaha
