About

Mike 'Murderer-behind-the-wheel' Fulham

There's only two things in life that Jack Blogger holds dear; the first is his license plate, and the second is the sweet satisfaction of nursing a cold brew during a tough drive. He is an avid collector of the former, scavenging through wreckages found at his local motorway junction. Throughout his years of vulturing, Jack has perfected a certain affinity for predicting the model of the vehicle involved in the wreckage, solely from the inscription on their license plate. One would assume those are two completely independent and unrelated qualities, but Jack manages anyway. He can even, on occasion, predict the exact colour of the vehicle too!
